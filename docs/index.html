Welcome to the official documentation site for the defense in Crown v Tia.

About

This repository contains comprehensive legal defense documentation for Crown v Tia, a criminal negligence case involving the accidental drowning of a child. Our efforts focus on establishing the mother's innocence using scientific evidence, legal precedents, and family dynamics.

Key Defense Arguments

  • Scientific Evidence: Timelines and analyses explaining the evidence.
  • Legal Precedent: Arguments regarding the standard of gross negligence.
  • Family Dynamics: Psychological analysis of familial patterns.
